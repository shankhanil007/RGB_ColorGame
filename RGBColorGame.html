HOW TO PLAY?

  • Red, Green, Blue are the three primary colours. By mixing them in various proportions we can get all the different colours.
  • In this game the amount of Red, Green, Blue in an UNKNOWN colour is given in the Format RGB(0-255, 0-255, 0-255).
    ( R- RED, G- GREEN, B- Blue ) Where 0-255 indicate the amount of RGB present in the colour.
  • Your task is to figure out which colour it is. Click on the right colour!!
